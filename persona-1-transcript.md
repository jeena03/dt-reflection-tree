# Persona 1 — External, Entitlement, Self-focused

A user who had a difficult day, felt controlled by circumstances, and focused more on what they didn’t receive.

---

Q: How would you describe your day today?  
A: Tough and frustrating  

Q: Today was tough and frustrating — what was at the heart of it?  
A: I couldn't solve an issue that needed solving  

Q: Whatever happened today — what guided your actions in that situation?  
A: I felt unsure and didn't take much action  

Q: Thinking about today as a whole — how do you feel about the role you played in how it went?  
A: It was hard to do much — the situation was working against me  

→ Axis 1 Result: External  

---

Q: Think of a moment today where you had to put in effort. What mattered most to you in that moment?  
A: That my effort is noticed by the right people  

Q: Looking back at that same moment — how do you feel about how you showed up?  
A: I feel like the effort I put in deserved more recognition  

Q: Was there a moment today where you could have helped someone — but didn't?  
A: Yes — I felt it wasn't really my responsibility  

→ Axis 2 Result: Entitlement  

---

Q: When you think about the most important moment of your day — who comes to mind first?  
A: Just me and my own work  

Q: In that moment — what shaped your reaction the most?  
A: My own pressure and workload  

Q: Did you check in on anyone today — even briefly?  
A: No — I was too caught up in my own work  

→ Axis 3 Result: Self  

---

## Final Reflection

Today you leaned toward feeling shaped by circumstances around you, noticing what you were expecting back, and staying focused on your own responsibilities. You described your day as 'Tough and frustrating'. That's one data point. Tomorrow is another.
